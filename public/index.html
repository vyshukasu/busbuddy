About TravelBus

Your Trusted Travel Partner

TravelBus is India's leading online bus ticketing platform that has transformed the way millions of Indians travel. With our website and mobile app, you can book bus tickets for any route across the country with just a few clicks.

Founded in 2013, we have revolutionized bus travel by bringing the entire process online, from selecting routes to making payments. We work with over 2,000 bus operators, covering more than 100,000 routes, and service nearly 20 million customers.

Our mission is to make travel accessible, affordable and convenient for everyone. We leverage technology to provide a seamless booking experience, along with features like live tracking, rating & reviews, and 24/7 customer support.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a bus ticket on TravelBus?

▼

Booking a ticket on TravelBus is easy. Simply enter your source, destination, and date of journey in the search box. Select your preferred bus from the list of available options, choose your seat, fill in passenger details, and make the payment. Your ticket will be sent to your email and mobile number.

Can I cancel my bus ticket? What is the refund policy?

▼

Yes, you can cancel your ticket from your account section. The refund amount depends on the time of cancellation and the cancellation policy of the bus operator. Generally, cancellations made 24 hours before departure get a higher refund percentage. The refund is processed within 5-7 business days.

How can I get a discount on my bus ticket?

▼

We regularly offer discount codes and promotions that you can apply during checkout. You can find these on our "Offers & Deals" section. Additionally, we have special discounts for first-time users, group bookings, and senior citizens. You can also earn and redeem loyalty points for additional savings.

Is it safe to travel during COVID-19?

▼

We are working closely with bus operators to ensure all safety protocols are followed. Buses are regularly sanitized, passengers are required to wear masks, and social distancing measures are implemented where possible. We recommend checking the latest safety guidelines before your journey.

How can I contact customer support?

▼

You can contact our 24/7 customer support team via the help bot on our website, by calling our toll-free number at 1800-123-4567, or by emailing us at support@travelbus.com. We typically respond to all queries within 2 hours.
